Squircle · matched silhouette
The squircle is a real superellipse: |x/a|n + |y/b|n = 1. With n = 5, it matches the iOS app-icon silhouette — curvature blends continuously (G2) instead of switching abruptly from edge to arc.
When to use which. Use r-* rounded corners for surfaces, cards, buttons and inputs — the simpler arc reads cleaner at small radii. Use squircles for icon containers and brand marks (the Gal logo chip, app icons, integration tiles), where the continuous curvature makes the silhouette feel softer and more confident at uniform scales.
